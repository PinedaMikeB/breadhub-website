The Best Bakery in Taytay, Rizal

Welcome to BreadHub Taytay, your premier destination for freshly baked artisan breads and classic Filipino favorites. Located in the heart of Taytay, Rizal, we take pride in delivering warm, delicious baked goods straight from our ovens to your table.

Our Story

BreadHub was founded with a simple mission: to bring back the authentic taste of traditional Filipino breads while embracing modern flavors. Every morning, our skilled bakers prepare each item using time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients, ensuring that every bite brings you the comfort and joy that only fresh bread can provide.

What Makes Us Special

At BreadHub, we believe that great bread starts with quality ingredients. We source locally whenever possible, supporting Filipino farmers and producers. Our Product Range

We offer an extensive selection of baked goods to satisfy every craving. Our donuts come in various flavors, from classic glazed to creative combinations.
